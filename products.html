Agriculture is the practice of cultivating crops and rearing animals for food, fiber, medicinal plants, and other products.

Major Agricultural Products

These can be broadly grouped into:

-Foods: Including cereals (grains), vegetables, fruits, cooking oils, meat, milk, eggs, and fungi.

-Fibers: Such as natural fibers used in textiles.

-Fuels: Biomass for energy.

-Raw Materials: For example, rubber.
