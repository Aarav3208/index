Rural Development

  • Objective: SARD aims to increase food production sustainably and enhance food security.
  • Approach:
    • Education initiatives
    • Economic incentives
    • Development of appropriate technologies
    • Ensuring stable supplies of nutritious food
    • Access for vulnerable groups
    • Production for markets
    • Employment and income generation
    • Natural resource management and environmental protection
  • Challenges:
    • Slow progress in sustainable agriculture and rural development worldwide.
    • Unfulfilled goal of global food security.
